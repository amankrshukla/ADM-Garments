Our Story

A quieter way to dress

ADM Garments began with a simple question: what would menswear look like if it were made for restraint, not attention?

We work with a small handful of family-run mills across Portugal and Japan, choosing fewer fabrics and holding each one to a higher bar. Every piece is designed to be worn quietly, for years — not posted once and forgotten.

I.

Honest Fabric

Natural fibers, sourced from mills we visit ourselves each season.

II.

Restrained Design

Nothing loud. Silhouettes that flatter and disappear into daily life.

III.

Considered Pricing

Sold direct, priced for the making — not a name on a label.

The Workshop

Small runs.
Made with care.

We produce in small batches, checked by hand before they ship. It costs more. We think it shows in how a garment wears, not just how it looks on day one.
